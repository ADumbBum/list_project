BURGER RECIPIE


ingridients

  • burger patty
  • two pices of bread
  • ketchup
  • oil

instructions

Step one is to pour oil onto pan. Step two is to wait till the oil starts popping. Step three is to put the patty on the same pan.
Step four is to wait till the patty has become brown to your liking. Step five is to put the patty in between the slices of bread.
Step six is to add ketchup between those same slices. Step seven is to enjoy in good health and moderation!
